Frequently asked questions

Here's what you need to know about Alfheim's tiers, based on the questions we get asked the most.

At Alfheim, any payment is a SINGLE-TIME payment, there are no renewal or subscription plans.

The supporter is not a tier on Alfheim, but a supporter program for those who want to support Alfheim's development and help the community to grow.

In case of a support payment submission, you will be able to request a refund within the following 30 days from the date of payment. In case it's a tier upgrade payment, then you will be able to request a refund as long as you ain't made any publications on Alfheim.

In case you fulfill the refund request requirements, you can submit the request of payment refund at alfheim9community@gmail.com.

Unless you want to support the community, or become a Member of Alfheim, you DO NOT need to make any payment and enjoy the Visitor Account features for free and forever on Alfheim
